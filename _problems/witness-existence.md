---
layout: problems
name: Witness Existence
input: >
  Given a program $\Pi(x, w)$, an input $x$ and an integer $t$ given in unary.
question: >
  Does there exists a $w$ of size at most $t$ such that
  $\Pi(x, w)=\mathsf{yes}$ after $t$ or fewer steps?
reference: [M&M Ch.5]
tags: [NP-complete]
---
